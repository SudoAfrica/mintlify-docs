## Onboarding checklist

1. Confirm your institution account and environment access.
2. Collect your `institutionId` and authentication credentials.
3. Create your OAuth application.
4. Generate an OAuth token.
5. Test utility endpoints in sandbox.
6. Move to card and user flows after authentication is stable.
