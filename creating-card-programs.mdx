## Overview

Card creation depends on the card program configured for your institution.

## Before you create cards

Make sure you know:

- The card program identifier
- The card scheme
- The product rules for issuance
- Any encryption requirements tied to secure operations

Store the resulting `cardProgramId` so you can reuse it during card creation flows.
