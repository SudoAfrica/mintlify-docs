## Overview

Before you call the API, make sure your institution has been onboarded and your team has the required access.

## You will need

- An active institution account
- Access to the Cardcore dashboard or admin workflow
- Your `institutionId`
- A valid institution token or OAuth credentials

If you do not have these yet, contact your Cardcore support or onboarding contact.
